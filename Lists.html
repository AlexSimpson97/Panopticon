Angela's Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 1/2 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/2 teaspons of yeast
  • 1/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 3 cups of Brea flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and to the mixture
  3. Add the flour and mix until combinedn into a dough
  4. Kneed the dough for 10 minutes
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Leave it somewhere to rise for hours
